## Recitals

H4H engaged KTS to provide technical development services in connection with The Commons, a social media and community platform operated by H4H as part of the MountainShares program. In the course of that engagement, KTS developed the Artificial Learning & Location Intelligence System ("ALLIS"), an independent platform for locally hosted intelligence, geospatial services, and community connectivity.

The parties enter this Agreement to clearly document the nature of their relationship, the ownership of work product and intellectual property created during and after that engagement, the terms under which H4H is licensed to use ALLIS within its ecosystem, and the sponsorship arrangement under which H4H makes ALLIS-powered services available to community partners at no cost.

---

## Section 1 — Definitions

**"ALLIS"** means the Artificial Learning & Location Intelligence System, including all software, models, architectures, protocols, data pipelines, node configurations, deployment tooling, documentation, and derivative works, developed by KTS independently or in the course of services provided to H4H.

**"The Commons"** means the social media and community marketplace application operated by H4H as part of the MountainShares program.

**"MountainShares"** means the community rewards program, DAO governance structure, and associated instruments owned and operated by H4H.

**"MS. Designation"** means the prefix "MS." applied to an ALLIS node (e.g., MS.NPS.ALLIS, MS.THURMOND.ALLIS), indicating that the node is deployed under the MountainShares Sponsorship Tier made available through H4H's sponsorship of the ALLIS platform.

**"MountainShares Sponsorship Tier"** means the no-cost community deployment tier of ALLIS, made available to eligible community partners because H4H sponsors those deployments as part of its public-benefit mission.

**"Services"** means technical development, installation, maintenance, and related work performed by KTS for H4H under this Agreement or any Statement of Work issued hereunder.

**"Statement of Work" or "SOW"** means a written document executed by both parties describing a specific scope of services, deliverables, timeline, and compensation.

**"Work Product"** means deliverables created by KTS specifically for H4H under a signed SOW, expressly designated as work-for-hire in that SOW.

## Section 2 — IP Ownership Clarification

### 2.1 ALLIS Is Owned by KTS

ALLIS is an independent platform owned solely by Kidd's Technical Services. It was conceived, designed, and built by KTS. The fact that ALLIS was developed during a period in which KTS was also providing services to H4H does not transfer, assign, or encumber KTS's ownership of ALLIS.

H4H has no ownership interest in ALLIS, in any component of ALLIS, or in any derivative works of ALLIS, except as expressly granted in Section 3 of this Agreement.

### 2.2 Work-for-Hire Scope Is Limited

Only deliverables expressly designated as "work-for-hire" in a signed Statement of Work are owned by H4H. No such designation applies to ALLIS or to any component of ALLIS. The absence of a written work-for-hire designation for ALLIS confirms KTS's ownership.

### 2.3 The Commons Deliverables

Specific application features, content, and configurations delivered by KTS to H4H under signed SOWs and designated as work-for-hire belong to H4H. These deliverables are separate from ALLIS and do not include the ALLIS platform, its underlying architecture, models, or intelligence layer.

### 2.4 H4H Content

Content, data, community materials, and institutional intellectual property contributed by H4H to any system or platform remain owned by H4H. KTS has no ownership claim over H4H content.

## Section 3 — License Grant from KTS to H4H

### 3.1 License

KTS grants H4H a non-exclusive, non-transferable, royalty-free license to use ALLIS within The Commons ecosystem and the MountainShares program for the duration of this Agreement, subject to the terms herein ("H4H License").

### 3.2 Scope of H4H License

The H4H License permits H4H to:
- Deploy ALLIS-powered features within The Commons application
- Reference ALLIS capabilities in MountainShares program documentation and partner communications
- Make ALLIS-powered services available to MountainShares community partners under the MountainShares Sponsorship Tier (MS. Designation nodes), subject to Section 4

The H4H License does not permit H4H to:
- Sublicense, resell, or transfer ALLIS to any third party independently of KTS
- Modify, reverse-engineer, or create derivative works of ALLIS without KTS's written consent
- Represent ALLIS as a product or service owned by H4H
- Deploy ALLIS outside The Commons and MountainShares ecosystem without KTS's written consent

### 3.3 Attribution

H4H agrees to accurately represent the ownership of ALLIS in all partner-facing documentation, program materials, and public communications. All such materials shall identify ALLIS as a platform owned and operated by Kidd's Technical Services, deployed within the MountainShares ecosystem under a sponsorship arrangement.

## Section 4 — MountainShares Sponsorship Tier

### 4.1 Sponsorship Arrangement

H4H sponsors the deployment of ALLIS nodes for eligible community partners — including municipalities, public safety agencies, federal agency partners, and nonprofit organizations — under the MountainShares Sponsorship Tier. Sponsored nodes carry the MS. Designation (e.g., MS.NPS.ALLIS).

### 4.2 What Sponsorship Means

H4H's sponsorship means:
- H4H covers the cost of those deployments as part of its public-benefit mission and program budget
- Community partners receive ALLIS-powered services at no cost to them
- KTS installs, operates, and maintains sponsored nodes under the terms of this Agreement and applicable SOWs
- The MS. Designation signals to partners and the public that the node is operating under H4H's sponsorship umbrella

### 4.3 What Sponsorship Does Not Mean

H4H's sponsorship of a node does not:
- Transfer ownership of ALLIS or any node to H4H or to the community partner
- Grant the community partner any license to ALLIS beyond use of the services provided at that node
- Create any obligation for KTS to provide services beyond what is documented in a signed SOW
- Prevent KTS from deploying ALLIS independently for paying clients outside the MountainShares ecosystem

### 4.4 Independent ALLIS Deployments

KTS retains the right to deploy ALLIS for clients outside the MountainShares ecosystem under direct commercial agreements. Such deployments do not carry the MS. Designation and are not governed by MountainShares program documents. H4H has no claim on revenues or relationships arising from independent ALLIS deployments.

## Section 5 — Services and Compensation

### 5.1 Statements of Work

All services performed by KTS for H4H under this Agreement are governed by signed Statements of Work. Each SOW specifies scope, deliverables, timeline, compensation, and any work-for-hire designations.

### 5.2 Compensation for Sponsored Nodes

KTS's compensation for installing and operating MS. Designation nodes is governed by individual SOWs negotiated between the parties. H4H's sponsorship commitment covers KTS's agreed costs for those nodes. Nothing in this Agreement obligates H4H to fund node deployments beyond what is specified in a signed SOW.

### 5.3 Independent Node Economics

KTS sets its own pricing for independent (non-MS.) ALLIS deployments. H4H has no interest in, and no right to, revenues from those deployments.

## Section 8 — Term and Termination

### 8.1 Term
This Agreement is effective on the date signed by both parties and continues until terminated by either party.

### 8.2 Termination for Convenience
Either party may terminate this Agreement with sixty (60) days written notice. Termination does not affect obligations under active SOWs, which continue through their specified completion dates unless separately terminated.

### 8.3 Effect of Termination
Upon termination:
- The H4H License in Section 3 terminates, and H4H shall cease deploying or promoting ALLIS within its ecosystem
- KTS shall complete or wind down active SOW obligations as agreed
- Each party returns or destroys the other's confidential information
- Active MS. Designation nodes transition per a written wind-down plan agreed by both parties

### 8.4 Survival
Sections 2 (IP Ownership), 6 (Representations), 7 (Confidentiality), and 9 (General Provisions) survive termination.
