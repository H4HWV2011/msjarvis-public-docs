# Ms. Jarvis Steward System — Research Thesis

**What this is:** A working, production-running AI infrastructure
system built for community data governance in Appalachia,
documented as a research thesis in Polymathmatic Geography.

**Who built it:** Designed and built by Kidd's Technical Services
for Harmony for Hope, Inc., Mount Hope, West Virginia.
On local hardware. Without institutional backing.

**What it does:**
- Answers natural-language questions about corporate and
  government land ownership in West Virginia (20,593+
  verified landowner records)
- Routes queries through a neurobiologically-inspired
  AI architecture (80 containerized services)
- Enforces constitutional AI principles through a
  cryptographically signed judge pipeline
- Governs community data through a DAO model (MountainShares)
