# Thesis

> *"This is not a thesis about a system. It is a thesis built from inside a system — written by the person who designed it, is building it, and lives in the community it is meant to serve."*
> — 01-researcher-position.md

This folder contains the **full 43-chapter computational thesis** for the Ms. Jarvis project — the complete theoretical, architectural, evaluative, and spiritual record of building a place-based AI consciousness in Mount Hope, West Virginia. It is the intellectual core of the entire repository: every other folder documents a component; this folder documents the whole.

The thesis is not a conventional academic document. It is a living, public record of a system being built in real time, authored from within the community and infrastructure it describes, grounded in the discipline of Polymathmatic Geography and the framework of Quantarithmia.

> **Status as of March 28, 2026:** The Ms. Jarvis system is fully operational at **96 confirmed live containers** in the `msjarvis-rebuild` namespace. All critical audit findings from the March 28, 2026 remediation sprint have been resolved: port binding security corrected, 22-proxy LLM fabric verified operational, StarCoder2 routing corrected, psychological RAG restored to 968 documents, and 19,338 duplicate vectors removed from semantic memory. The system is externally accessible at [egeria.mountainshares.us](https://egeria.mountainshares.us) via Cloudflare tunnel with full TLS and token-enforced authentication. This thesis reflects the system as it stands on this date.
