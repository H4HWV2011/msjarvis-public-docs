# Legal Documents Overview (Index)

This repository contains two primary end‑user legal documents related to MountainShares and The Commons:

- **MountainShares Rewards Program – Terms and Conditions** (`legal/Terms and Conditions.md`)  
- **The Commons – Terms of Use** (`legal/Commons Terms of Use.md`)

These documents are designed to work together:

- The **Rewards Program Terms** govern your participation in the MountainShares rewards program and your use of MountainShares units (PMS and EMS).  
- The **Commons Terms of Use** govern your access to and use of The Commons application and website, including social, marketplace, and content features that exist whether or not you use MountainShares.

In any direct conflict about MountainShares, the Rewards Program Terms control. In any direct conflict about general Platform use, the Commons Terms control, subject to Applicable Law.
