## Appendix B: Definitions

- **EMS Hour:** One verified hour of qualifying community work, logged in the recognition ledger with UEI, geo, org, and timestamp. Permanent, non-transferable, non-spendable as recognition units. Securities-law treatment under review by securities counsel; this document makes no representation as to whether EMS is or is not a security.
- **Rank:** A participant's standing in the contribution game, calculated automatically from cumulative EMS hours and tenure. Cannot be purchased or assigned.
- **Badge:** A permanent visual record of a specific contribution achievement. Phase-colored, shape-categorized, IPFS-anchored.
- **Groundbreaker:** Phase 0 rank — the only rank that is time-gated to a specific program phase. Cannot be earned after Phase 1 opens.
- **Founding Legacy:** The rarest badge — requires both Phase 0 Groundbreaker status and Champion rank. Cannot be manufactured or replicated.
- **Participation Weight (PW):** Phase 3 dividend allocation score — Verified Months Active × Activity Multiplier. Distinct from rank.
- **Founding Deep:** Highest Participation Weight tier — 61–84 verified active months; 2.0× multiplier.
- **FVSR (Volunteer Time Value Rate):** The Independent Sector / Do Good Institute Value of Volunteer Time — a BLS-derived rate published annually by Independent Sector and the Do Good Institute. Not a federal or IRS rate. Current 2026 rate: $36.14.
- **Verification Veteran:** Badge earned by responding to three or more Ms. Allis data verification ping cycles — recognizing the ongoing stewardship of living place data.
- **Regional Champion (Tier 3):** A Phase 2 governance role, eligible at Gold rank, anchoring county-level activation in the regional corridor network.
- **Hard Trigger:** Automatic system rule enforced by the Phase Management Controller. Non-overridable by any governance vote or rank level.
- **Three-Box Structure:** Entity wall separating H4H (charitable anchor), MountainShares Commons/DUNA (community ledger), and KTS (founder commercial engine). Rank and badges do not modify this structure.
