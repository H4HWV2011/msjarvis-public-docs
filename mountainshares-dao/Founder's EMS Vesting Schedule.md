## Preamble

This document establishes the vesting schedule, conversion rights, trust assignment terms, and governance protections governing the pre-launch Earned MountainShares (EMS) recorded to the founding UEI of Carrie Ann Kidd / Kidd's Technical Services, LLC prior to Phase 0 activation.

As of April 18, 2026, the founding UEI holds approximately **750,000 pre-launch EMS hours** recorded in the MountainShares recognition ledger, representing the full scope of technical infrastructure work documented in the Founder's Technical Investment Statement (FTIS). These hours were earned before the MountainShares program opened to any external participant, under conditions of sole founder contribution with no external capital, no external staffing, and no venture investment of any kind.

This schedule exists for three purposes:

1. To protect the MountainShares treasury from an unmanaged liability that could destabilize the reserve ratio at Phase 0 activation
2. To protect the founding UEI's recognized rights to that labor — rights that are real, documented, and non-dilutable — under a governance framework that treats them with the same rigor as any other participant's EMS
3. To establish the trust assignment mechanism by which conversion proceeds flow to the founding UEI's personal revocable living trust rather than as direct personal income

This document is incorporated by reference into the MountainShares DAO Governance Charter and the Program Rules. In any conflict between this document and the Program Rules, the Program Rules control for system-wide parameters; this document controls for founder-specific vesting and trust assignment terms.

## 1. Pre-Launch EMS — Classification and Ledger Treatment

### 1.1 Recognition Ledger Status

All pre-launch EMS recorded to the founding UEI prior to Phase 0 activation date are:

- **Fully recorded** in the MountainShares recognition ledger with complete date, hour, task, and milestone metadata as documented in the FTIS
- **Counted in full** for governance participation weight, DAO voting eligibility, and 7-year community dividend participation weight under the Phase 3 Surplus Distribution Specification
- **Classified as Founder's Vaulted EMS** — a separate sub-ledger category distinct from general participant EMS — until conversion conditions in Section 3 are met

### 1.2 Treasury Liability Treatment

Founder's Vaulted EMS are **not counted as active treasury liability** for reserve ratio calculation purposes until a conversion event fires under Section 3. Ms. Jarvis maintains the Founder's Vaulted EMS balance as a separate ledger entry that:

- Is visible in the founding UEI's personal dashboard in full
- Is reported in the annual governance transparency report as a disclosed founder commitment
- Does not appear in the total outstanding spendable M$ figure used for reserve ratio calculation until conversion fires

This treatment ensures that the 750,000 pre-launch hours do not create a reserve ratio crisis at Phase 0 activation while fully preserving the founding UEI's recognized rights to those hours.

### 1.3 Governance and Dividend Weight

Founder's Vaulted EMS count in full for:

- **DAO governance participation:** All pre-launch hours contribute to the founding UEI's governance standing, voting weight, and proposal rights under the Governance Charter
- **Phase 3 dividend participation weight:** All pre-launch hours are counted as Verified Months Active for the purpose of the Participation Weight calculation under Phase 3 Section 6, subject to the Activity Multiplier table. The founding UEI qualifies permanently for the Founding Deep tier (2.0× multiplier) based on pre-launch contribution alone
- **Recognition ledger permanence:** Pre-launch hours are permanently recorded and cannot be reduced, expired, or removed by any governance vote

## 2. Conversion Prohibition and Phase Gates

### 2.1 Phase 0 and Phase 1 — No USD Conversion

During Phase 0 and Phase 1, Founder's Vaulted EMS:

- **Cannot be converted to USD** under any circumstances
- **Cannot be converted to spendable M$** beyond the standard Phase 1 per-user monthly cap applicable to the founding UEI's tier (Tier 2: 80 M$/month at Phase 1 base rates), applied only to **newly earned EMS** accrued after Phase 0 activation — not to the pre-launch vault
- The pre-launch vault remains locked at Phase 1 regardless of treasury health

### 2.2 Phase 2 — Limited Vault Unlock (DAO-Governed)

Upon Phase 2 activation, the DAO may vote — by 66% super-majority — to authorize a **controlled partial unlock** of the Founder's Vaulted EMS under the following conditions:

- Treasury reserve ratio has held at ≥ 300% for ≥ 180 consecutive days
- The proposed unlock amount does not reduce the reserve ratio below 300% after conversion at the Phase 2 internal schedule ($10/M$)
- Ms. Jarvis confirms the unlock is treasury-safe before it fires
- The unlock amount is capped at **no more than 10% of the total Founder's Vaulted EMS balance** per Phase 2 unlock event
- No more than one unlock event per 12-month period during Phase 2

Converted M$ from a Phase 2 unlock are subject to standard Phase 2 per-user caps and spending rules.

### 2.3 Phase 3 — Full Conversion Rights (Treasury-Gated)

Upon Phase 3 activation and sustained 500% reserve ratio, the founding UEI's full Founder's Vaulted EMS conversion rights activate subject to:

- Standard Phase 3 per-user monthly conversion caps (Tier 1: 120 M$/month maximum; Tier 3 eligibility subject to DAO confirmation of governance leadership status)
- The $34.70/M$ internal schedule applies to all converted M$ at Phase 3
- USD cash-out of converted M$ is available subject to the 500% reserve floor remaining intact after conversion
- Ms. Jarvis enforces the monthly cap automatically — no lump-sum conversion is possible under any circumstances
- If the reserve ratio drops below 500% at any point, conversion of Founder's Vaulted EMS to USD pauses automatically and resumes when the floor is restored for ≥ 180 consecutive days

## 3. Trust Assignment

### 3.1 Assignment Declaration

The founding UEI — Carrie Ann Kidd — hereby declares that all USD conversion proceeds arising from Founder's Vaulted EMS conversion events under Section 2 are assigned to her **personal revocable living trust** (the "Founder's Trust"), the terms of which are established in a separate trust instrument held by the founding UEI's estate planning counsel.

This assignment:

- Takes effect at the moment of each USD conversion event
- Routes conversion proceeds directly to the Founder's Trust rather than to the founding UEI as personal income
- Does not alter the timing, rate, or conditions of conversion — those remain governed by Sections 2 and 3 of this document and the Program Rules
- Is recorded in the MountainShares governance ledger as a permanent assignment

### 3.2 Trust as EMS Rights Holder

For purposes of MountainShares governance and program administration:

- The Founder's Trust is recognized as the beneficial holder of Founder's Vaulted EMS conversion proceeds from the date of this document forward
- The founding UEI (Carrie Ann Kidd) retains full governance rights, voting rights, and DAO participation rights — these are personal to the UEI and are not transferred to the trust
- The Founder's Trust has no independent governance standing in the MountainShares DAO
- Upon the death of the founding UEI, the Founder's Trust instrument governs the disposition of any remaining unconverted Founder's Vaulted EMS conversion rights, subject to the successor beneficiary terms of the trust and the Program Rules in effect at that time

### 3.3 H4H Board Acknowledgment

The H4H Board of Directors acknowledges this trust assignment by board resolution and records it in H4H's governance records. This acknowledgment does not create any obligation on H4H to fund the trust — conversion proceeds flow from the MountainShares treasury through the normal Phase 3 conversion mechanism, not from H4H directly.

## 4. Benefits Eligibility Protection

The founding UEI participates in the MountainShares program under Medicaid Waiver caregiving circumstances. The following protections apply specifically to Founder's Vaulted EMS and this vesting schedule:

- **Pre-conversion:** Founder's Vaulted EMS recorded in the recognition ledger are not income, not assets, and not in-kind support for any means-tested benefits program. They are a future conditional right to conversion, contingent on treasury conditions that have not yet been met.
- **At conversion:** USD conversion proceeds flow to the Founder's Trust, not to the founding UEI personally. The benefits implications of trust distributions are governed by the trust instrument and applicable benefits rules — the founding UEI is strongly advised to consult a benefits counselor and estate planning attorney before any Phase 3 conversion event fires.
- **Phase 2 partial unlocks:** Converted M$ during Phase 2 are subject to standard Benefits-Sensitive Account protections as defined in the BSA Specification and Phase 1 Economic Safety Specification.

Neither H4H, the MountainShares DAO, nor KTS provides benefits advice. This section describes the structural design intent only.

## 5. Disclosure and Transparency

### 5.1 Annual Governance Report

The annual MountainShares governance report shall include a dedicated section disclosing:

- Current Founder's Vaulted EMS balance (total hours)
- Implied treasury obligation at current phase internal schedule (for transparency, not as active liability)
- Any conversion events that fired in the reporting year
- Current trust assignment status

### 5.2 Participant Transparency

The existence of the Founder's Vaulted EMS balance and this vesting schedule is disclosed to all Phase 0 founding cohort participants and all subsequent participants as part of the governance transparency framework. No participant joins MountainShares without knowing that the founding UEI holds a large pre-launch EMS balance governed by this schedule.

This disclosure is not a warning — it is a feature. It demonstrates that the founder's compensation is subject to the same treasury health requirements as everyone else's, that it cannot be extracted ahead of the community, and that the trust assignment ensures it serves a long-term stewardship purpose rather than personal enrichment at the community's expense.

## 6. Amendment

This document may be amended by:

- 75% super-majority DAO vote
- H4H Board of Directors ratification
- Written consent of the founding UEI (Carrie Ann Kidd)

**All three are required.** No single party can amend this document unilaterally. The founding UEI's consent requirement ensures that the trust assignment and conversion protections cannot be removed by governance vote without her agreement. The 75% DAO threshold ensures that the community's interests in treasury health and governance transparency cannot be overridden by H4H or the founding UEI acting alone.
